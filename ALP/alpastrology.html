A System, Not Just Intuition.

Where traditional Vedic astrology times events using Dasha/Bhukti (Moon-based periods), ALP progresses the Lagna (Ascendant) instead. We advance one Nakshatra Pada every 1 year, 1 month, and 10 days to pinpoint life events with remarkable consistency.

Structured & Learnable

ALP is taught like a science. Because it relies on mathematical progression rather than pure intuition, anyone can learn to apply it accurately.
