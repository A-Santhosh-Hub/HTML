Methodology & Scope

This audit is based on a direct, live review of 12 pages: Home, About, Courses, Consultation, Services, Contact, Articles, Videos, Events, Testimonials, Software, and Books — cross-checked against the screenshots supplied — plus external verification of the brand's Google Play listing and the competitive landscape of Vedic astrology education platforms in India.

Honest limitations, stated upfront so nothing here is overstated: no mobile screenshots were available (mobile findings are flagged as recommendations for direct QA, not measured claims); no PageSpeed/Lighthouse, Google Search Console, or analytics access was available (performance findings are structural risk factors, not measured scores); the live contact/enquiry form was not submitted (so backend delivery wasn't tested); and JavaScript-rendered widgets (review carousel, stat counters) were inspected via their static HTML, which is also exactly what a slow connection or strict crawler would see.

Brand & Business Snapshot

What ALP is:

Akshaya Lagna Paddhati (ALP) — also called Age Lagna Paddhati — is a predictive astrology method invented by Dr. S. Pothuvudaimoorthy, Ph.D., a fourth-generation astrologer. Where traditional Vedic astrology times events using Dasha/Bhukti (Moon-based), ALP progresses the Lagna (Ascendant) instead, across a 120-year span at a rate of one Nakshatra Pada per 1 year, 1 month, 10 days — giving (per the brand's claim) more consistent, reproducible timing across astrologers who use the method.

  • Founder: Dr. S. Pothuvudaimoorthy — also trained in Pranic Healing, Reiki, and Past Life Regression; featured on Vijay TV, Zee Tamil, Jaya Plus, Vendhar TV, and Pudu Yugam TV.
  • Co-founder: Smt. Arularasi Pothuvudaimoorthy — D.T.Ed., M.Sc., B.Ed.; runs ALP's administrative and publishing operations.

Metrics & Proof

  • Claimed stats: 50,000+ clients, 300+ star reviews, 25+ years experience, 20+ services.
  • Verified external proof: ALP's Google Play app has 100,000+ downloads and a 4.13/5 rating from 792 reviews — genuinely strong, independently-verifiable social proof that the website currently does nothing with.

Actual revenue lines identified (4, not 2):

  1. 1-to-1 Consultations
  2. Courses — Basic / Advanced / Master (3 levels)
  3. Software — ALP Basic / Advanced / TP Professional (Windows) + Android/iOS app
  4. Books — 6 volumes by the founder, ₹450–₹750 each

Only 2 of these 4 (Consultation, Courses) appear in the main navigation.

Phase 1 — Website Analysis (Page by Page)

Audit

Home

Strengths: Strong hero (clear positioning line, dual CTA, credibility trio: "ALP Method / 3 Levels / 1-to-1"), logical section flow (Who We Are → Courses → Consultation → Services → Stats → Contact → Testimonials), confident dark starfield hero against warm cream body sections.
Weak/Missing: The stat counters render as "0 +" in static HTML. Testimonials carousel is completely empty in static HTML. Software and Books are absent from the page's core value proposition. No media/press bar despite five real TV features.

About

Strengths: Genuinely good founder story — the "banyan tree" origin narrative is distinctive and human. Clear Vision/Mission split. Co-founder is named and credentialed, which most competitor sites skip entirely.
Weak/Missing: Zero supporting proof for the credentials and TV features (no logos, clips, or certificate scans). Pranic Healing / Reiki / Past Life Regression are mentioned but never become an actual service — a dangling content thread.

Courses

Strengths: Clean 3-level structure (Basic → Advanced → Master) with topic bullets per level, "Popular" badge on Level 1.
Critical gaps: Zero pricing anywhere. Zero duration, zero start/batch dates, zero certificate visual, zero instructor info, zero downloadable syllabus, zero per-course testimonials. "Enroll Now" on all three levels scrolls to one generic enquiry form — there is no way to signal which level you're interested in.

Consultation

Strengths: Genuinely good "How it Works" 3-step flow. Clear guidance categories.
Critical gaps: The booking form asks for Name, Phone, City, Message — not Date, Time, or Place of Birth. Every single lead requires manual back-and-forth. Also: icon images load from a third-party prototyping domain (`creative-code-truth.lovable.app`) — a real reliability risk.

Infrastructure Issues

  • Services: This page is a verbatim duplicate of the homepage's Services section. Adds no unique content.
  • Contact: Phone link correctly uses `tel:`. But no embedded Google Map. No business hours. The footer's phone/email point to `#`.
  • Resource Hub:
    • Articles: All 6 posts dated identically (June 12, 2026), no internal links forward.
    • Videos: Mislabeled. Contains zero embedded videos, just text articles.
    • Events: Promises "upcoming events," delivers recap-style blog posts with no dates/calendar.
    • Testimonials: Dedicated page has no testimonials in static HTML.
  • Footer (site-wide defect): Every page's "Quick Links" use relative hrefs without a leading slash. On `/courses/`, the footer "Home" links to `/courses/index.html` (404). All social icons are placeholder `#` links across the entire site.

Hidden, unlinked pages:

  • /software/ 3 paid Windows tiers + app. The "Get on Play Store" buttons are dead `#` links. Every "Download" button sends the identical WhatsApp message referencing "ALP Advanced".
  • /books/ 6 titles with real pricing. The most commerce-complete page on site, yet not in main nav. Every "Buy Now" button sends the identical message for "Book 1".
  • /faq/ Referenced only in broken footer links. Functionally invisible.

Device Rendering:

Mobile: Priority QA needed for hamburger menu, WhatsApp bubble overlap, hero wrapping, and iOS Safari auto-zoom on forms.

Desktop: Site's clearest strength. The visual identity (dark starfield, warm cream, amber CTA) is genuinely more polished and intentional than most competitor sites. The design foundation is good; problems are entirely structural.
